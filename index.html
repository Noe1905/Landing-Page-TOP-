Discover São Paulo: The Heart of Brazil

São Paulo, a bustling metropolis, offers a vibrant blend of culture, history, and innovation. It’s a city that never sleeps, filled with countless opportunities for exploration and discovery.

Some random information

São Paulo is the largest city in Brazil and the Southern Hemisphere, with a population of over 12 million people.

The city is home to the largest Japanese community outside of Japan.

São Paulo hosts the world's largest Pride Parade, attracting millions of visitors every year.

The São Paulo Art Museum (MASP) houses one of the most important collections of Western art in Latin America.

São Paulo is the place where all Brazilians come to reinvent themselves.

-Caetano Veloso, Brazilian musician and cultural icon.
